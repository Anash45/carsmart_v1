Here are some of the types of insurances you may have.

Legally required insurances:

Employers' liability insurance:
  • Coverage: Required by law, it covers claims for injuries or illnesses suffered by employees while they are working.
  • Importance: Ensures compliance with legal requirements and protects against employee-related claims.
Motor insurance (Vehicle Fleet Insurance):
  • Coverage: Covers all vehicles used by the transport service provider under one policy.
  • Importance: Provides comprehensive coverage for the fleet used in transporting vehicles, including damage and theft.

Recommended insurances:

Goods in transit insurance:
  • Coverage: Protects against damage, loss, or theft of vehicles while they are being transported.
  • Importance: Essential for covering the value of the vehicles being transported in case of an accident or other incidents.
Public liability insurance:
  • Coverage: Covers claims for injury or damage to third parties or their property caused by the transport provider's operations.
  • Importance: Provides protection against legal claims arising from accidents or damage involving the transport service.

Additional advisable insurances you may have:

Professional indemnity insurance:
  • Coverage: Protects against claims of negligence or errors in professional advice or services provided.
  • Importance: Provides protection against legal claims arising from accidents or damage involving the transport service.
Breakdown recovery insurance:
  • Coverage: Provides cover for recovery costs if a vehicle in the transport provider's fleet breaks down.
  • Importance: Ensures that any breakdowns during transport do not lead to additional costs or delays.
Cargo insurance:
  • Coverage: Specific coverage for the goods (in this case, vehicles) being transported.
  • Importance: Ensures that any damage to the vehicles being transported is covered, often included as part of Goods in Transit insurance but may be separately specified.
Contractual liability insurance(if applicable):
  • Coverage: Covers liabilities arising from specific contractual obligations related to vehicle transport.
  • Importance: Important if the transport provider has contractual commitments that require additional coverage beyond standard policies.
